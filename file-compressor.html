Supported Compression Formats

  • ZIP: A widely-used format that works on almost every operating system. Great for general-purpose file compression.
  • GZIP: Commonly used for compressing large text files or logs, particularly on UNIX-based systems.
  • 7ZIP: Known for its high compression ratio and ability to handle large files. Ideal for users who need efficient compression.
  • TAR.GZ: Often used in Linux and UNIX environments. It’s a compressed archive format that combines multiple files into one.
